Frequently Asked

How do I earn money on AdMinterz?
Watch short 30-second ads to earn 5 points each. Reach 5,000 points and withdraw $5.00 in USDT or Bitcoin. Up to 10 ads per day on the Starter plan.
Is AdMinterz available in my country?
Yes — AdMinterz is available worldwide. Payouts are in cryptocurrency (USDT or BTC), so anyone with a crypto wallet can withdraw regardless of country.
Where does the money come from?
Ad revenue. Advertisers pay to show their ads to our members. That revenue funds your points payout. No pyramid, no returns — just real ad money.
What is the $5 membership fee for?
It's a one-time platform access fee — not an investment. It unlocks your daily ad-watching features. Members who invest something are more engaged.
How fast are withdrawals processed?
Within 24–48 hours after you submit your request. We verify each withdrawal manually to prevent fraud and protect all members.
Can I refer friends?
Yes! Earn 500 points for every friend who joins using your referral link. There's no limit to how many friends you can refer.
